Jointer Plane

The jointer plane is huge! My favorite hand tool by far though. When making panels this tool is excellent. The long bed allows you to slice off peaks on a boards edge with ease. It is also fantastic at flattening large projects such as a tabletop, or even your workbench. When working on any long edge this is the tool to grab.
