Bushcraft Skills

Discover the art of wilderness living at our bushcraft camp. Learn essential survival skills, explore traditional crafts, and reconnect with nature in a serene forest setting. Whether you're a beginner or seasoned adventurer, join us to master the craft of thriving off the land.

  1. Fire Craft

    22 December 2023 By: Trevor Ferro, Flint, Tinder 53 Comments

    Master the elemental skill of fire-making with our comprehensive guide to Fire Craft. Learn traditional techniques like flint and steel, bow drilling, and modern methods using ferrocerium rods. This section delves into the science of fire, the selection of ideal tinder, and the construction of reliable fire structures to ensure you can ignite and maintain a warming flame under any wilderness condition.

  2. Essential Gear

    31 December 2023 By: Trevor Cordage, Knives, Tools 122 Comments

    Explore Essential Gear, where we detail the must-have tools and equipment for any bushcraft enthusiast. From multi-functional knives and durable backpacks to specialized cooking gear and water purification systems, we cover the essentials that ensure safety, efficiency, and sustainability in the wild. Understand how to choose the best quality gear that won’t let you down when you’re far from the beaten path.

  3. Shelter

    23 October 2023 By: Trevor Tarps, Tents, Cordage 286 Comments

    Discover the art of constructing protective structures in nature. Whether it’s a simple lean-to, a debris hut, or an advanced tarp setup, learn how to use the natural environment to your advantage. This guide provides step-by-step instructions on building various shelters that can withstand the elements, ensuring a safe and comfortable wilderness experience.

  4. Navigation

    31 January 2023 By: Trevor Compass, Maps 39 Comments

    Navigate the wilderness like a pro with our practical tips on bushcraft navigation. This article covers the essentials of using a topographic map, compass, and natural navigation aids such as stars and tree moss. From reading landscapes to understanding magnetic declination, equip yourself with the skills to traverse any terrain without losing your way.
